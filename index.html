{BugHunters - Coding Club}

Welcome to BUG HUNTERS, the premier coding club at our college dedicated to fostering a passion for programming, problem-solving, and innovation. Our club brings together students who are eager to explore the world of coding, debug complex challenges, and collaborate on exciting tech projects.

{What We Do}

Weekly coding sessions and problem-solving meetups Guest lectures and workshops by industry professionals Participation in inter-college coding contests and hackathons Group projects and open-source contributions Networking events and career guidance

{Why Join BUG HUNTERS?}

Enhance coding and problem-solving abilities Build a strong professional network Gain hands-on experience through projects and competitions Access to exclusive resources and learning materials Be part of a vibrant, supportive tech community

{Get Involved}

Everyone with an interest in coding is welcome. Join BUG HUNTERS to embark on a journey of learning, growth, and innovation. Together, let's hunt down bugs and build the future!
